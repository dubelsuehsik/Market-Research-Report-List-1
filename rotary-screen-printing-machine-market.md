<p><p>The rotary screen printing machine market comprises three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require skilled operators to guide the printing process, offering flexibility but lower productivity. Semi-automatic machines enhance efficiency by automating specific tasks while still needing some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines provide the highest level of automation, allowing for high-volume production and minimal human oversight, thus improving speed and consistency. Each type caters to different production needs and budget constraints in the textile industry.</p></p>
