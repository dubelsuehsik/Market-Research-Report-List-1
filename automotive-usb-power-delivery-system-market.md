<p><p>The Automotive USB Power Delivery System market is primarily divided into two types: USB Type-A and USB Type-C. USB Type-A connectors are the traditional standard, widely used in older vehicles for charging and data transfer, providing limited power output. In contrast, USB Type-C has gained popularity for its reversible design and higher power delivery capabilities, allowing for faster charging and supporting a broader range of devices. The shift towards USB Type-C is driven by its versatility and increasing consumer demand for efficient charging solutions.</p></p>
