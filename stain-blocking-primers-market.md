<p><p>The stain-blocking primers market features two main types: oil-based and water-based primers. Oil-based stain-blocking primers are known for their superior adhesion and durability, making them ideal for heavy stains and challenging surfaces, but they require longer drying times and have strong odors. In contrast, water-based stain-blocking primers are more environmentally friendly, quicker to dry, and easier to clean up, although they may be less effective on certain tough stains. Each type caters to different needs in home improvement and renovation projects.</p></p>
