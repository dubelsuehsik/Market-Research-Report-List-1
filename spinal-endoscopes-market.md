<p><strong>The Spinal Endoscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Far Lateral or Horizontal</li><li>Posterior or Interlaminar</li><li>Posterolateral or Transforaminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal endoscope market comprises three main types: **far lateral or horizontal**, which allows for access to lateral intervertebral discs and foramina; **posterior or interlaminar**, which provides access through the midline of the spine for decompression and disc removal; and **posterolateral or transforaminal**, which targets specific spinal nerves and disc herniations from a lateral approach. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, enabling surgeons to perform minimally invasive procedures with improved precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
