<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in the pharmaceutical industry for their precise fluid handling capabilities. Roller peristaltic pumps utilize rotating rollers to compress tubing, creating a controlled flow of fluids while minimizing contamination risks. Shoe-type peristaltic pumps, on the other hand, use a shoe mechanism to achieve similar compression, offering gentle handling of sensitive fluids. Both types are widely used for transferring pharmaceuticals, ensuring accurate dosing and maintaining the integrity of the products during processing and packaging.</p></p>
