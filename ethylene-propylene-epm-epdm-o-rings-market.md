<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene O-Rings market encompasses two primary types: EPM O-Rings and EPDM O-Rings. EPM O-Rings are based on ethylene-propylene monomer and are known for their excellent flexibility and resistance to heat and oxidation, making them suitable for a variety of applications. EPDM O-Rings, derived from ethylene propylene diene monomer, offer superior resistance to weathering, aging, and chemicals. Both types are widely used in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors for sealing solutions due to their durability and performance characteristics.</p></p>
