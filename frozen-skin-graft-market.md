<p><strong>The Frozen Skin Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split-thickness</li><li>Full-thickness</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen skin graft market is primarily divided into two types: split-thickness and full-thickness grafts. Split-thickness grafts involve the epidermis and a portion of the dermis, making them suitable for larger wounds and areas requiring more flexibility. Full-thickness grafts, which encompass the entire dermis and epidermis, are ideal for smaller, more specific areas, offering better cosmetic outcomes and durability. Both types are crucial in reconstructive surgeries, burn treatment, and chronic wound management, catering to various patient needs.</p></p>
