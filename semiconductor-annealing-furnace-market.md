<p><p>The semiconductor annealing furnace market is categorized into three key types: vertical furnaces, horizontal furnaces, and others. Vertical furnaces are designed to optimize thermal processes by allowing for efficient gas flow and temperature uniformity, making them suitable for high-throughput applications. Horizontal furnaces, on the other hand, facilitate easier loading and unloading of wafers, providing flexibility in processing. "Others" encompass specialized and emerging furnace designs that cater to niche applications or advanced materials, addressing specific manufacturing requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
