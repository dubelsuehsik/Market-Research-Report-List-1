<p><strong>The IV Solutions in Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Solutions</li><li>Hypotonic Solutions</li><li>Hypertonic Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV solutions in containers market consists of isotonic, hypotonic, and hypertonic solutions, each serving distinct clinical purposes. Isotonic solutions maintain fluid balance, ideal for hydration and electrolyte replenishment. Hypotonic solutions facilitate cellular hydration by allowing fluid influx, crucial for conditions like dehydration. Hypertonic solutions drive fluid movement out of cells, used to reduce cerebral edema or expand blood volume. This diverse range caters to various medical needs, contributing to effective patient care in healthcare settings.</p></p>
