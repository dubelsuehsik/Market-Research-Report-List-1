In contrast, multi-core cables feature multiple conductors packaged together, allowing for enhanced flexibility and the capability to transmit various signals simultaneously. Both types are crucial for electric vehicle systems, providing essential connections for battery management, power distribution, and motor operations, contributing to overall vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
