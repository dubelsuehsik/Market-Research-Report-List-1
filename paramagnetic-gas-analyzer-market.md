<p><p>The paramagnetic gas analyzer market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Thermal Conductivity Type**: This method measures gas concentration based on thermal conductivity differences between the gas and a reference. </p><p>2. **Thermal Magnetic Type**: This type exploits the magnetic properties of gases under various temperatures, providing precise measurements.</p><p>3. **Electrochemistry Type**: Utilizing electrochemical reactions, this analyzer detects specific gas concentrations through electrodes, offering sensitivity and specificity, particularly for hazardous gases. Each type serves distinct applications based on the required measurement accuracy and environmental conditions.</p></p>
