<p><p>The single-stage thermal power steam turbine market comprises three primary types: condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing turbines convert exhaust steam into water, maximizing energy efficiency by using low-pressure steam. Back pressure turbines, on the other hand, utilize exhaust steam for process heating, offering efficient energy recovery in industrial applications. "Others" may include specialized turbine designs catering to specific operational requirements or unique energy systems. Each type serves distinct purposes in power generation and industrial processes, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
