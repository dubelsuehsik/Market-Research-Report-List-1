<p><p>The PE substrate siliconized film market is categorized into three main types based on the polyethylene used: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) siliconized film, LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) siliconized film, and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) siliconized film. LDPE siliconized films are known for their flexibility and moisture resistance, making them suitable for a variety of applications. LLDPE films offer enhanced strength and puncture resistance, while HDPE films provide superior rigidity and durability, catering to specific packaging and industrial needs in the market.</p></p>
