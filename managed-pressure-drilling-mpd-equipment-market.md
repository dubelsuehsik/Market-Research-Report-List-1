Conventional MPD utilizes standard drilling techniques enhanced by pressure management to optimize performance, primarily in traditional reservoirs. In contrast, unconventional MPD focuses on advanced techniques suited for challenging environments, such as shale gas or deepwater drilling, where unpredictable pressure variations necessitate specialized equipment.
