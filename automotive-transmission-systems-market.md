<p><p>Automotive transmission systems are critical for vehicle performance, with key types including manual transmission and double-clutch transmission. Manual transmission involves the driver manually changing gears, offering greater control and engagement, often preferred by enthusiasts. In contrast, double-clutch transmission automates gear shifts using two separate clutches for odd and even gears, resulting in quicker and smoother transitions. This advanced system enhances fuel efficiency and driving comfort, making it popular in performance and luxury vehicles. These transmission types cater to diverse driving preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>
