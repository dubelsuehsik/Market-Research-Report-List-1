<p><strong>The Optical Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multispectral Imaging</li><li>Electro optical Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fingerprint sensor market includes two primary types: multispectral imaging and electro-optical imaging. Multispectral imaging captures multiple wavelengths of light to create detailed fingerprint images, effectively penetrating skin to enhance accuracy, especially for dirty or worn fingerprints. Electro-optical imaging, on the other hand, uses light sensors to convert fingerprint patterns into electrical signals, providing clear and precise fingerprint data. Both technologies aim to improve security and authentication processes across various applications, including mobile devices and biometric security systems.</p></p>
