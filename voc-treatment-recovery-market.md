<p><p>The VOC Treatment & Recovery market includes various technologies for managing volatile organic compounds. Regenerative Thermal Oxidation (RTO) and Recuperative Thermal Oxidation (RTO) utilize heat to destroy VOCs, while Catalytic Oxidation enhances oxidation at lower temperatures using catalysts. Photo-oxidation relies on light to induce reactions. Adsorption by Activated Carbon captures VOCs, and Rotor Concentrators pre-concentrate emissions before treatment. Cryocondensation cools to condense VOCs for recovery. Each method addresses specific industrial needs for VOC management and environmental compliance.</p></p>
