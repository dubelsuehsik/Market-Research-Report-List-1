<p><p>The Specialty Absorptive Dressings market includes two main types: Non-Adhering Dressings and Self-Adhering Dressings. Non-Adhering Dressings are designed to protect wounds without sticking to the healing tissue, minimizing pain during dressing changes and reducing trauma. In contrast, Self-Adhering Dressings feature an adhesive layer that securely holds the dressing in place over the wound, offering convenience and stability. Both types play crucial roles in managing exudate and supporting optimal wound healing while catering to different patient needs.</p></p>
