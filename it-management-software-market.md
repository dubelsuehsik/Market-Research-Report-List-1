Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and offers flexibility, scalability, and often requires no local installation. SaaS (Software as a Service) refers to subscription-based software delivered over the internet, providing users with regular updates and maintenance without the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Each type caters to varying business needs and deployment preferences.</p></p>
