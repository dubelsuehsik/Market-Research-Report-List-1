<p><p>A Polymer Stent Graft is a medical device designed to treat vascular diseases by supporting and reinforcing weakened or damaged blood vessels. Made from synthetic polymers, these stent grafts offer enhanced flexibility, compatibility, and durability compared to traditional metal stents. They are primarily used in procedures involving aneurysms, lesions, or strictures, allowing for improved blood flow and reduced risk of complications.</p><p>The Polymer Stent Graft Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in stent graft technology, and the rising geriatric population susceptible to vascular ailments. Moreover, there is a growing preference for minimally invasive surgical techniques, which boosts the demand for stent grafts. </p><p>Current trends include the development of bioresorbable stent grafts and the integration of drug-eluting technologies to minimize restenosis rates. Manufacturers are focusing on enhancing material properties to provide better biocompatibility and reduce inflammatory responses.
