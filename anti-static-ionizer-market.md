<p><strong>The Anti Static Ionizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type Ionizer</li><li>Induction Type Ionizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-static ionizer market is segmented into two main types: contact type ionizers and induction type ionizers. Contact type ionizers operate by directly applying ionized air onto surfaces, effectively neutralizing static charges through physical contact. In contrast, induction type ionizers generate ions without direct contact, using a high-frequency electric field to produce ionized air that neutralizes static electricity from a distance. Both types are essential for industrial, electronic, and cleanroom applications, ensuring safe and efficient operations in static-sensitive environments.</p></p>
