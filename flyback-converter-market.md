<p><p>The Flyback Converter market comprises two primary types: isolated and non-isolated converters. Isolated Flyback Converters provide electrical isolation between the input and output, enhancing safety and reducing noise, making them suitable for applications like power supplies in telecom and industrial sectors. Non-isolated Flyback Converters, on the other hand, do not offer such isolation, which allows for simpler designs and lower costs, often used in consumer electronics and low-power applications. Both types are essential in various industrial and commercial power needs.</p></p>
