<p><strong>The Rolling Stock Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rolling stock wheel market is primarily divided into two types: casting and forging. Cast wheels are produced by pouring molten metal into molds, resulting in complex shapes at lower production costs. Conversely, forged wheels are made by shaping heated metal under high pressure, providing superior strength and durability. Both types serve different applications in rail transport, with casting often used for lighter trains and forging preferred for heavier loads due to its enhanced performance characteristics.</p></p>
