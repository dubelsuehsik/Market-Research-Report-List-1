<p><p>The polyurethane insulation board market primarily includes two types: Expanded Polystyrene Foam (EPS) and Extruded Polystyrene Foam (XPS). EPS is lightweight, cost-effective, and provides good thermal insulation, often used in walls and roofs. XPS, on the other hand, offers enhanced moisture resistance and strength, making it suitable for below-grade applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for energy-efficient building materials, supporting sustainable construction practices while improving thermal performance in residential and commercial structures.</p></p>
