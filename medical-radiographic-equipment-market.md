<p><ul><li>CR Technology</li><li>DR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical radiographic equipment market primarily consists of Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR) technologies. CR technology uses photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, which are then digitized using a scanner, making it a cost-effective option for many facilities. In contrast, DR technology directly converts X-ray images into digital format through sensors, providing faster image acquisition and superior image quality. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
