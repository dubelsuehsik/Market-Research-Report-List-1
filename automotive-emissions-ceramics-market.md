<p><p>The automotive emissions ceramics market includes three main types: honeycomb, gasoline particulate filters (GPF), and diesel particulate filters (DPF). Honeycomb ceramics are used in catalytic converters and provide a large surface area for chemical reactions, enhancing emissions reduction. GPFs target gasoline engine emissions by capturing particulates, improving air quality. DPFs are designed for diesel engines, trapping soot and preventing it from entering the atmosphere. Together, these ceramic technologies contribute significantly to reducing automotive emissions and meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>
