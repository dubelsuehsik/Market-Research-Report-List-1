<p><ul><li>Low Power Rating</li><li>High Power Rating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive busbar market is categorized into low power rating and high power rating segments. Low power rating busbars are designed for applications requiring less than 100 amps, commonly used in electrical distributors and smaller electronic components within vehicles. In contrast, high power rating busbars handle currents exceeding 100 amps, typically utilized in high-demand systems such as electric vehicle drivetrains and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). These distinctions help manufacturers optimize performance and safety in automotive electrical systems.</p></p>
