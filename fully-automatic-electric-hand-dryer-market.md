<p><p>The fully automatic electric hand dryer market comprises two main types: Jet Air Type and Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type. Jet Air Type dryers use high-speed airflow to quickly remove moisture, often resulting in lower energy consumption and quicker drying times. In contrast, Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type dryers utilize heated air to evaporate water from hands, typically taking longer to achieve dryness. Both types focus on enhancing hygiene and convenience in public restrooms while contributing to environmental sustainability.</p></p>
