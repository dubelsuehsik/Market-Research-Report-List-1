<p><strong>The Shock Absorbers for Railways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axle Box Shock Absorber</li><li>Pillow Damper (Central Damper)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway shock absorbers market primarily consists of axle box shock absorbers and pillow dampers (central dampers). Axle box shock absorbers are designed to mitigate vibrations and shocks at the wheelset's axle, enhancing ride comfort and stability. In contrast, pillow dampers provide central damping by absorbing vertical and lateral forces, improving overall vehicle dynamics. Both types play crucial roles in reducing wear and tear on railway infrastructure, ensuring smoother operations and extending the lifespan of rolling stock.</p></p>
